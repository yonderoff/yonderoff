Moores Cove

User Review


- K-MIC(B0zz$)

Moore's Cove is one of our favorite hikes. The drive to the cove doesn't take very long from Asheville (~40 minutes) and the hike is appropriate for young and old. We have taken 70+ year old grandparents and 3 year old kiddos on this trail. There are several fun bridges to cross, some streams to splash in and a wonderful water fall that you can eat lunch behind. This is the hike we take nearly all visitors to when they come to visit us in Asheville. On your way in/out, be sure to also stop at Looking Glass Falls. Enjoy!
