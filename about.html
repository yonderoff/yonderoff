YonderOff had its genesis at Asheville Startup Weekend 2013. We have an awesome team!

Heather Selzer

Heather has a genuine passion for community service and empowering others with knowledge. With more than 10 years of experience in Asheville in the real estate industry, from marketing, sales, training and managing large number of agents, her unique background allows her to guide others through the complexities of many types of problem-solving. She grew up in an family of entrepreneurs and is always looking for needs to be filled.

Using her skills as a highly proficient project and team manager, Heather also works with area non-profits and campaigns, with a focus on both greenways and diversified economic development, to help sustain Asheville as a model community.
